Frequently asked questions
How do I create a shipping label?
Go to Ship in the sidebar. Enter the recipient's address, choose your package type and weight, then compare carrier rates. Click Buy label to purchase — the label is instantly available for download or printing.
What carriers do you support?
We support USPS, UPS, and FedEx. Rates are sourced in real time via EasyPost, so you always see the latest discounted prices sorted cheapest first.
How long is a purchased label valid?
Labels are valid for 28 days from the purchase date. If a label is unused within that window, you can void it to receive a refund to your wallet.
Can I print a label on a regular printer?
Yes. Choose 8.5×11 PDF format when purchasing and print on any standard laser or inkjet printer. For thermal printers (Zebra, Rollo, etc.) choose 4×6 ZPL format instead.
How do I add funds to my wallet?
Go to Wallet in the sidebar. Select a preset amount ($10–$250) or enter a custom amount, then click Add to my wallet. Funds are added instantly via Stripe — all major cards accepted.
Do wallet credits expire?
No. Wallet credits never expire regardless of your plan. They remain available as long as your account is active.
Can I get a refund of my wallet balance?
Wallet balance is non-refundable to your original payment method. However you can use all credits to purchase labels at any time. If you believe a charge was made in error, contact support below.
How do I void a label?
Go to Label History, find the label, and click the expand arrow. Then click Void label. Once approved, the refund is credited to your wallet within 1–2 business days. Labels can only be voided if not already scanned by the carrier.
How long does a void refund take?
USPS voids are typically processed within 2–5 business days. UPS and FedEx voids are usually same-day. Refunds are credited to your wallet, not your original payment method.
What format should my CSV be in?
Download our CSV template from the Batch Upload page. Required columns: name, street, city, state, zip, weight_oz, service. Optional: company, phone, length, width, height.
What happens to rows with errors?
Error rows are skipped and not charged. After purchase, you'll see a summary of failed rows with the specific error for each. You can fix them and re-upload or use the inline Fix button in the results screen.
Is batch upload a Pro feature?
Yes. Batch CSV upload (up to 500 rows) is available on the Pro plan only. Upgrade to Pro →
